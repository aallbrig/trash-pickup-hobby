Trash Pickup Hobby

Getting Started

Trash pickup grabber. This item will let you grab trash in hard to reach areas like in or under bushes.

Prepare yourself for a nice forearm workout as you repeatedly squeeze the handle.

Sometimes makes more sense to bend down to grab some entrenched litter. For that reason (and more) you'll want to make sure your hands are protected.

I recommend grabbing a pair of gloves that someone in construction would use.

This bright yellow vest will make you visible while working.

When I first started this hobby I would carry my trashbag with me. Unfortunately that limited the amount of litter I could pick up to what I could carry. With this wagon I can gather so much more refuse on the side of the road.

I recommend this item so that it is more comfortable to walk from litter pile to litter pile.

You never know what type of trash you will encounter. For that reason I recommend a very sturdy bag.

Don't let sharp objects like broken glass obliterate the integrity of your bag. Use a trash bag that is designed for more heavyduty workloads.

Preparation

You (probably) aren't able to throw away all the trash you collect in your own trash container. You will have to find a way of disposing of a bunch of bags of trash.

I recommend spending some time researching locations of usable dumpsters.

I have asked some local businesses if I can throw away bags of trash in exchange for keeping their grounds clean. If you do this please don't be a jerk and fill up their entire dumpster space with your bags!

My Approach

I think it's important to have fun and to track progress. Anything you can do it keep yourself returning to the hobby is encouraged.

I like to keep a total count of how many bags of trash I've created. I feel a sense of pride having my count.

Don't spend too much time being frustrated by the trash. It is depressing how some places have been overrun by trash. Focus on how much better a spot looks after you've worked your magic.

It is nice to listen to podcasts, music, and/or audiobooks.

I usually spend about 30 minutes to an hour collecting enough trash to fill up an entire bag.

I will take the trash bags back to my garage. Once I have three or four filled up I'll put them in my car and drive them to a dumpster.

I really enjoy seeing the space after I've cleaned it. It fills me with satisfaction!

Next Steps

Place an order for some equipment. Get outside and find areas you want to clean up.

Most of all make sure to have a good time!
